Privacy Policy — Timevera

Last updated: 3 July 2026

Timevera ("the app") is a workforce time-tracking application provided to employees of the businesses that subscribe to it. This policy explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

Information we collect

  • Account information — your name and email address, used to sign you in.
  • Timesheet & attendance data — the hours, projects, and work locations you log.
  • Clock-in photo (selfie) — a photo captured with your camera when you clock in, used by your employer to verify attendance.
  • Location — your GPS location at the moment you clock in, used to confirm you are on the job site. Location is only read at clock-in, not tracked continuously.
  • Profile photo — an optional image you choose from your photo library.
  • Push notification token — used to send you approval and reminder notifications.

How we use your information

Your information is used solely to provide the time-tracking service to you and your employer: recording hours, verifying attendance, calculating payroll, and sending you notifications. We do not sell your personal information or use it for advertising.

Who can see your data

Your timesheets, attendance, photos, and location are visible to the administrators of your employer's account. Data is stored securely with our infrastructure provider (Supabase) and is not shared with third parties except as required to operate the service.

Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your employer maintains their account, or until you or your employer request its deletion.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting your employer's administrator or us at the email below.
