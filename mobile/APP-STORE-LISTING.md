## Description
Timevera is the mobile companion for construction and trades teams to track their work hours accurately.

Clock in and out with a photo and GPS location so your attendance is verified on site. Log timesheets by project and work location, request overtime with a reason, and see your history — including your daily clock-in photos and true worked time down to the minute.

FEATURES
• Clock in / out with selfie + location verification
• Log timesheets by project (site or workshop)
• Request overtime with approval tracking
• View your work history and hours (shown as “8h 30m”, not decimals)
• Get notified when your overtime or attendance is approved
• Daily clock-out reminders
• Secure sign-in with Face ID / fingerprint
• Change your password and profile photo in Settings

Timevera is provided to employees of businesses that subscribe to the service. You will need an account created by your employer to sign in.
